# WKU Software Crew Project - Sustainability Plan

## 1. Sustainability Vision

"Leverage technology to democratize education, minimize environmental impact, and create an inclusive community"

## 2. Economic Sustainability

### 2.1 Revenue Diversification

**Current (Year 1)**:
- Government grants: 55%
- B2B: 34%
- B2C: 6%
- Other: 5%

**Target (Year 3)**:
- B2B: 50%
- B2C: 17%
- Government grants: 14%
- Other: 19%

→ Reduce grant dependency, achieve self-sufficiency

### 2.2 Cost Optimization

**Cloud Costs** (₩50M → ₩30M via):
- Reserved instances (30% savings)
- Spot instances for dev/test
- Caching optimization
- Resource right-sizing

**Personnel** (Smart hiring):
- Remote work (save office costs)
- Part-time/contractors for peak needs
- Automate repetitive tasks

## 3. Environmental Sustainability

### 3.1 Green IT

**Renewable Energy**:
- AWS committed to 100% renewable energy by 2025
- Choose data centers powered by renewables

**Energy Efficiency**:
- Serverless architecture (Lambda, Fargate)
- Optimized code (reduce compute time)
- CDN to reduce long-distance transfers

### 3.2 Carbon Footprint

**Goal**:
- 2026: Measure carbon emissions
- 2027: Carbon neutral

**Method**:
- Use Cloud Carbon Footprint tool
- Offset via carbon credits (trees, renewable projects)

### 3.3 Paperless Operations

- All documents digital (Google Workspace)
- Electronic signatures (DocuSign)
- Online meetings (Zoom)
- No printed marketing materials

## 4. Social Sustainability

### 4.1 Education Equity

**Free Access**:
- Junior Class completely free
- No credit card required

**Scholarships**:
- Low-income students: Premium free (50/year)
- Top performers: Pro free (10/year)
- Underrepresented groups: Case-by-case

**Accessibility** (WCAG 2.1 AA):
- Screen reader support
- Keyboard navigation
- Subtitles on all videos
- High contrast mode

### 4.2 Diversity & Inclusion

**Team Diversity**:
- Gender balance (50% women in leadership by Year 3)
- No age/education discrimination
- Diverse backgrounds valued

**Content Diversity**:
- Feature diverse role models
- Women in tech, disabled developers
- LGBTQ+ friendly community

**Community**:
- Zero tolerance for discrimination
- Active moderation
- Inclusive language

### 4.3 Community Impact

**University Partnerships**:
- Provide free/discounted platform access
- Support computer science education

**Volunteering**:
- Teach coding at high schools (monthly)
- Mentoring underrepresented students

**Local Economy**:
- Hire locally (Jeollabuk-do)
- Support local businesses

## 5. Governance

### 5.1 Ethics Code

**Core Values**:
- **Integrity**: Transparent, honest operations
- **Respect**: Value all stakeholders
- **Responsibility**: Fulfill social obligations
- **Innovation**: Continuous improvement

**Prohibited**:
- Corruption, bribery
- Discrimination
- Environmental harm
- User data misuse

### 5.2 Board of Directors

**Composition** (Year 2+):
- CEO (1)
- Investor representatives (2)
- Independent director (1)

**Responsibilities**:
- Oversee management
- Strategic decisions
- Risk management
- Ethics compliance

## 6. Transparency

### 6.1 Annual Report

**Published**: March each year  
**Contents**:
- Financial performance
- User statistics
- ESG achievements
- Future plans

**Distribution**: Website, email to stakeholders

### 6.2 ESG Report

**Published**: Biennial (every 2 years)  
**Contents**:
- **Environmental**: Carbon emissions, energy use
- **Social**: Education access, diversity metrics
- **Governance**: Board composition, ethics

**Framework**: GRI Standards or similar

## 7. Long-Term Vision (10 Years)

### 7.1 2035 Goals

**Users**:
- 1 million global users
- 50 countries

**Impact**:
- 1 million students taught coding
- 100K job placements
- 1,000 startups launched

**Environment**:
- Carbon negative (offset more than we emit)
- 100% renewable energy

**Social**:
- 10,000 scholarships provided
- All content free and accessible
- No ads (sustainable revenue model)

### 7.2 B Corp Certification

**Goal**: Achieve B Corporation status by Year 5

**Requirements**:
- Meet high standards of social/environmental performance
- Legal accountability to stakeholders (not just shareholders)
- Transparency

**Benefits**:
- Attract impact investors
- Brand credibility
- Talent attraction

## 8. Measurement & Reporting

### 8.1 ESG Metrics

**Environmental**:
- Carbon emissions (tCO2e)
- Energy consumption (kWh)
- % renewable energy

**Social**:
- Users served (free vs. paid)
- Scholarships granted
- Accessibility compliance score
- Diversity metrics (team, content)

**Governance**:
- Board independence
- Ethics training completion
- Transparency score

### 8.2 Quarterly Reviews

**Internal**: ESG committee meets quarterly  
**External**: Annual ESG report published
