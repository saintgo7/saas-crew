# WKU Software Crew Project - Customer Success Strategy

## 1. Customer Success Overview

### 1.1 Definition
Proactively helping customers achieve their desired outcomes while using our platform

### 1.2 Goals
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) > 70
- Week 4 retention > 30%
- Free-to-paid conversion > 10%
- Monthly churn < 5%

## 2. Customer Journey

### 2.1 Awareness
**Goal**: Make students aware of platform

**Tactics**:
- University partnerships
- Social media campaigns
- Word-of-mouth

### 2.2 Onboarding
**Goal**: Help users get first win quickly

**Tactics**:
- Welcome email (immediate)
- 5-minute tutorial ("Hello World" deployment)
- First course recommendation
- Checklist (3 tasks to complete)

**Success Metric**: Complete first lesson within 24 hours

### 2.3 Engagement
**Goal**: Build learning habit

**Tactics**:
- Weekly learning reports (email)
- Streak tracking (gamification)
- Push notifications (assignment due)
- Study group matching

**Success Metric**: Log in 3+ times per week

### 2.4 Conversion
**Goal**: Convert to paying customer

**Tactics**:
- Premium trial (7 days free)
- In-app upgrade prompts
- Success stories ("How Premium helped Minji land her dream job")
- Limited-time offers

**Success Metric**: 10% free-to-paid conversion

### 2.5 Retention
**Goal**: Keep users engaged long-term

**Tactics**:
- New content regularly
- Feature updates
- Community events (hackathons)
- 1:1 mentoring (Pro tier)

**Success Metric**: 12-week retention > 20%

### 2.6 Advocacy
**Goal**: Turn users into promoters

**Tactics**:
- Referral program (both get 1 month free)
- User success stories (interviews, videos)
- Ambassador program (10 students)

**Success Metric**: K-Factor > 0.5

## 3. Customer Segmentation

### 3.1 Segment: Beginners (Junior Class)

**Needs**: Simple, encouraging, guided

**Strategy**:
- Gamification (badges, levels)
- Short lessons (10-15 min)
- Supportive community
- Frequent positive reinforcement

**CS Actions**:
- Onboarding tutorial
- Study group auto-matching
- Celebrate first milestones

### 3.2 Segment: Intermediate (Senior Class)

**Needs**: Practical projects, feedback, portfolio

**Strategy**:
- Project-based learning
- Code reviews
- Portfolio generation
- Job prep

**CS Actions**:
- Recommend project ideas
- Connect with mentors
- Showcase projects publicly

### 3.3 Segment: Advanced (Master Class)

**Needs**: Mentorship, funding, business support

**Strategy**:
- 1:1 mentoring
- Investor intros
- Demo day opportunities
- Business resources

**CS Actions**:
- Dedicated account manager
- Quarterly check-ins
- Personalized roadmap

## 5. Proactive Customer Success

### 5.1 Health Score

**Calculation**:
- Login frequency (30%)
- Course progress (30%)
- Community engagement (20%)
- Assignment completion (20%)

**Scores**:
- 80-100: Healthy (Green) → No action needed
- 50-79: At Risk (Yellow) → Send re-engagement email
- 0-49: Critical (Red) → Personal outreach

### 5.2 Intervention Strategies

**Yellow (At Risk)**:
- Automated email: "We miss you! Come back and continue your learning"
- Personalized course recommendations
- Discount offer (20% off upgrade)

**Red (Critical)**:
- 1:1 email from CS team
- Phone call (Premium customers)
- Survey: "What can we improve?"
- Special offer (1 week free extension)

### 5.3 Success Milestones

**Celebrate wins automatically**:
- First lesson completed → Badge + email
- First course completed → Certificate + LinkedIn share
- 7-day streak → Congratulations email
- 100 XP earned → Level up notification

## 6. Customer Feedback

### 6.1 Collection Methods

**In-App Feedback**:
- Feedback button on every page
- Bug report form
- Feature request voting

**Surveys**:
- NPS survey (quarterly)
- Course satisfaction (after completion)
- Feature usage survey (monthly)

**Interviews**:
- 10-20 users per month
- 30-60 minutes
- Incentive: ₩20,000 Starbucks gift card

### 6.2 Feedback Loop

1. **Collect**: Via surveys, interviews, support tickets
2. **Analyze**: Categorize (bug, feature request, praise)
3. **Prioritize**: Impact vs. effort matrix
4. **Act**: Add to product backlog
5. **Close Loop**: Notify user when implemented

**Tool**: Canny or ProductBoard

## 7. Customer Success Team

### 7.1 Team Structure (Year 1)

**Customer Success Managers (CSM)**: 2
- Manage high-value accounts (universities, Pro users)
- Onboarding, training, renewals

**Support Specialists**: 2
- Handle support tickets
- Live chat
- Email support

**Community Manager**: 1
- Moderate forum
- Organize events
- Engage with community

### 7.2 CS Team KPIs

**CSM**:
- Customer retention > 90%
- Upsell rate > 10%
- NPS > 70

**Support**:
- Average response time < 5 min
- CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) > 4.5/5
- Ticket resolution rate > 95%

**Community Manager**:
- Forum engagement (posts/day)
- Event attendance
- Community NPS

## 8. Success Stories

### 8.1 Case Study Format
**Title**: "How Minji Went from Beginner to Intern at Samsung"

**Structure**:
1. **Background**: CS freshman, no coding experience
2. **Challenge**: Wanted to learn web development
3. **Solution**: Completed Junior → Senior classes on WKU Crew
4. **Result**: Built 5 projects, landed internship
5. **Quote**: "The platform made learning accessible and fun"

### 8.2 Distribution
- Blog post
- Social media (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Email newsletter
- In-app showcase

→ Goal: 1 success story per month

## 9. CS Playbooks

### 9.1 Onboarding Playbook
**Day 1**: Welcome email, tutorial prompt  
**Day 3**: Check-in email ("How's it going?")  
**Day 7**: First milestone celebration  
**Day 14**: Course recommendation  
**Day 30**: Upgrade prompt (Premium trial)

### 9.2 Churn Prevention Playbook
**Warning Signs**: No login for 7+ days, course progress stalled

**Actions**:
1. Send re-engagement email
2. Offer personalized help
3. Survey: "Why did you stop?"
4. Special incentive (discount, free month)

### 9.3 Upsell Playbook
**Trigger**: User completes Junior Class

**Pitch**:
- "Congrats on completing Junior! Ready for Senior?"
- Highlight Premium benefits (unlimited projects, certificates)
- Offer 7-day free trial
- Show success stories of Premium users
