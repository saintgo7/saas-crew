# WKU Software Crew Project - Market Analysis

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Market Overview

The global EdTech market is experiencing unprecedented growth, accelerated by digital transformation and the increasing demand for practical, skills-based education. The WKU Software Crew platform enters this market at an optimal time, positioned to capture the growing demand for integrated learning and startup support platforms specifically tailored for university students.

**Key Market Insights**:
- Global EdTech market projected to reach **$102B by 2030** (CAGR 16.3%)
- Korean EdTech market expected to reach **₩2T by 2030** (CAGR 18.2%)
- University students represent a **highly engaged, tech-savvy demographic**
- Increasing preference for **practical, project-based learning** over traditional lectures
- Growing **startup culture** among university students (25% consider entrepreneurship)

### 1.2 Market Opportunity

**Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: ₩1.2T
