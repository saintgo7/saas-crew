# WKU Software Crew Project - Expansion Strategy

## 1. Expansion Vision

### 1.1 5-Year Roadmap
**Year 1**: Wonkwang University + Jeollabuk-do (local dominance)  
**Year 2**: National expansion (10 universities)  
**Year 3**: 20+ universities, international pilot  
**Year 4**: Southeast Asia expansion  
**Year 5**: Global platform (100K+ users, 10 countries)

## 2. Geographic Expansion

### 2.1 Phase 1: Jeollabuk-do (Year 1)
**Target**: 3 universities, 1,000 users

**Universities**:
- Wonkwang University (500 users)
- Jeonbuk National University (300 users)
- Kunsan National University (200 users)

**Strategy**: Deep local focus, word-of-mouth

### 2.2 Phase 2: National Expansion (Year 2)
**Target**: 10 universities, 3,000 users

**Priority Regions**:
- Seoul (SKU, Hongik, Konkuk)
- Busan (Pusan National)
- Daegu (Kyungpook National)
- Gwangju (Chonnam National)

**Strategy**: University partnerships, national marketing

### 2.3 Phase 3: International (Year 3+)
**Pilot Markets**: Vietnam, Philippines, Indonesia  
**Rationale**: Large student populations, English-speaking, growing tech industries

**Localization**:
- Translate to English, Vietnamese, Tagalog
- Local payment methods
- Regional content (relevant examples)
- Local partnerships

## 3. Product Expansion

### 3.1 Horizontal Expansion (New Audiences)

**High School Students** (Year 2):
- Simplified curriculum
- AP Computer Science prep
- Coding clubs/competitions

**General Public** (Year 3):
- Career switchers
- Working professionals
- Non-CS majors (business, design)

**Corporate Training** (Year 3):
- B2B upskilling programs
- Custom corporate tracks
- Team management features

### 3.2 Vertical Expansion (Deeper Features)

**Job Platform** (Year 2):
- Job board (company postings)
- Resume builder
- Interview prep
- Job matching algorithm

**Freelance Marketplace** (Year 3):
- Students bid on projects
- Clients post gigs
- Platform takes 10% commission
- Reputation system

**Startup Accelerator** (Year 3):
- Cohort-based program (20 startups/year)
- $50K investment per startup
- 6-month intensive program
- Demo day with investors

## 4. Technology Expansion

### 4.1 Mobile Apps (Year 2)
**iOS and Android native apps**

**Features**:
- Mobile-optimized video player
- Offline course downloads
- Push notifications
- Mobile code editor (basic)

**Rationale**: 60% of users access on mobile

### 4.2 AI-Powered Features (Year 2)
- AI code assistant (GPT-4 integration)
- Personalized learning paths
- Automated code review
- Intelligent Q&A suggestions

### 4.3 AR/VR Learning (Year 4)
- VR coding environments
- Collaborative virtual classrooms
- 3D data visualization

## 5. Market Expansion Strategy

### 5.1 Market Entry Checklist
- [ ] Market research (size, competition, regulations)
- [ ] Localization (language, currency, payment)
- [ ] Local partnerships (universities, corporates)
- [ ] Compliance (data laws, business registration)
- [ ] Marketing strategy (channels, budget)
- [ ] Success metrics (users, revenue, NPS)

### 5.2 Go-to-Market Timeline (New Country)
**Month 1-2**: Research and planning  
**Month 3-4**: Localization and partnerships  
**Month 5-6**: Beta launch (100 users)  
**Month 7-12**: Scale (1,000 users)
