## 2. Investor Targeting

### 2.1 Angel Investors
**Profile**: Successful entrepreneurs, tech executives  
**Check Size**: ₩5M-₩20M  
**Value-Add**: Mentorship, network, credibility

**Target List** (10-15 angels):
- Former startup founders
- University professors (CS, Business)
- Tech company executives
- Serial angel investors in EdTech

## 3. Pitch Deck Structure

**15-20 Slides**:

1. **Cover**: Company name, tagline, date
2. **Problem**: Pain points of student developers
3. **Solution**: WKU Software Crew platform
4. **Product Demo**: Screenshots, video
5. **Market Size**: TAM/SAM/SOM
6. **Business Model**: Revenue streams
7. **Traction**: Users, revenue, growth
8. **Competition**: Competitive matrix
9. **Competitive Advantage**: Unique differentiation
10. **Go-to-Market**: Acquisition strategy
11. **Team**: Founders, key hires
12. **Financials**: 3-year projections
13. **Fundraising**: Amount, use of funds
14. **Vision**: 5-year roadmap
15. **Closing**: Contact, Q&A

## 5. Exit Strategy

### 5.1 Exit Options

**Option 1: IPO** (Year 7-10)
- KOSDAQ or NASDAQ listing
- Valuation: ₩100B+
- Requires: Strong revenue, profitability, governance

**Option 2: Acquisition** (Year 5-7)
- Strategic buyer (Naver, Kakao, Samsung)
- Valuation: ₩50B-₩200B
- Synergies with buyer's education initiatives

**Option 3: Lifestyle Business** (Ongoing)
- No exit, continue operating profitably
- Dividend payouts to shareholders
- Requires: Sustainable profitability

**Preferred**: IPO (maximize returns) or strategic acquisition (faster liquidity)
