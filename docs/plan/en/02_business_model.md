## 2. Revenue Model

### 2.1 Revenue Streams Breakdown

#### **Stream 1: B2C (Student Subscriptions) - 40% of Total Revenue**

**Three-Tier Pricing**:

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Free** | ₩0 | All students | - Junior class access<br>- Basic IDE<br>- Community Q&A<br>- 1 project deployment<br>- Public projects only |
| **Premium** | ₩9,900/mo | Active learners | - Senior class access<br>- Advanced IDE features<br>- 10 project deployments<br>- Private projects<br>- Priority support<br>- Certificate of completion |
| **Pro** | ₩29,900/mo | Serious developers & entrepreneurs | - Master class access<br>- Unlimited deployments<br>- Custom domains<br>- 1:1 mentoring (2hrs/mo)<br>- Startup resources<br>- API access<br>- Offline events |

**Annual Plans** (16% discount):
- Premium: ₩99,900/year (vs ₩118,800)
- Pro: ₩299,900/year (vs ₩358,800)

**Conversion Funnel**:
```
1,000 Free Users
    ↓ (10% conversion)
100 Premium Users (₩9,900 × 100 = ₩990,000/mo)
    ↓ (20% upgrade)
20 Pro Users (₩29,900 × 20 = ₩598,000/mo)

Total B2C MRR: ₩1,588,000
Annual: ₩19M (Year 1 target)
```

#### **Stream 2: B2B (University Licenses) - 35% of Total Revenue**

**Enterprise Packages**:

| Package | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | ₩10M/year | - Up to 500 students<br>- Standard features<br>- Quarterly reports<br>- Email support |
| **Professional** | ₩30M/year | - Up to 2,000 students<br>- Custom branding<br>- Dedicated account manager<br>- Monthly reports<br>- Priority support |
| **Enterprise** | ₩50M+/year | - Unlimited students<br>- White-label option<br>- Custom curriculum<br>- On-premise option<br>- 24/7 support<br>- SLA guarantees |

**Year 1 Target**:
- 1 university at ₩30M = ₩30M
- 5 departments at ₩10M = ₩50M
- **Total B2B**: ₩80M

#### **Stream 3: Corporate Partnerships - 15% of Total Revenue**

**Partnership Models**:

1. **Recruitment Partnerships** (₩5M-₩20M/year):
   - Exclusive job postings
   - Direct access to student profiles
   - Sponsored coding challenges
   - Campus recruiting events

2. **Sponsored Content** (₩2M-₩10M/campaign):
   - Company-branded courses
   - Tech talks and webinars
   - Logo placement
   - Newsletter sponsorship

3. **Talent Pipeline** (₩10M-₩50M/year):
   - Pre-screening services
   - Customized training programs
   - Internship coordination
   - Exclusive hiring rights

**Year 1 Target**:
- 3 recruitment partners: ₩30M
- 2 content sponsors: ₩10M
- **Total Corporate**: ₩40M

#### **Stream 4: Government Grants - 10% of Total Revenue**

**Grant Programs**:

1. **Ministry of Education**:
   - University Innovation Support: ₩50M-₩200M
   - Digital Education Initiative: ₩30M-₩100M

2. **SMBA (Small & Medium Business Administration)**:
   - Startup Support: ₩30M-₩100M
   - Youth Employment: ₩20M-₩50M

3. **Regional Development**:
   - Jeollabuk-do Innovation Fund: ₩20M-₩50M
   - Local Economic Development: ₩10M-₩30M

**Year 1 Target**: ₩178M (55% of total revenue)

## 4. Pricing Strategy

### 4.1 Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based Pricing**:
- Price based on value delivered, not just costs
- Students pay for outcomes (skills, projects, jobs)
- Universities pay for student success and retention
- Corporations pay for talent pipeline access

### 4.2 Competitive Pricing Analysis

| Competitor | Target | Price | WKU Software Crew |
|------------|--------|-------|-------------------|
| **Inflearn** | Individuals | ₩30,000-₩100,000/course | ₩9,900/mo (all courses) ✅ |
| **Elice** | Universities | ₩50M-₩200M/year | ₩10M-₩50M/year ✅ |
| **Programmers** | Job seekers | Free (ad-supported) | Freemium ✅ |
| **Codecademy** | Global | $19.99/mo | ₩9,900/mo ✅ |

**Competitive Advantages**:
- 67% cheaper than individual course purchases
- 60% cheaper than enterprise competitors
- More features than free alternatives

### 4.3 Pricing Experiments

**A/B Tests Planned**:

1. **Free Trial Duration**:
   - Option A: 7 days
   - Option B: 14 days
   - Option C: 30 days
   - Hypothesis: Longer trial → higher conversion

2. **Price Points**:
   - Premium: ₩7,900 vs ₩9,900 vs ₩12,900
   - Pro: ₩24,900 vs ₩29,900 vs ₩34,900
   - Hypothesis: ₩9,900 is optimal (psychological pricing)

3. **Annual Discount**:
   - 10% vs 15% vs 20% discount
   - Hypothesis: 16% maximizes annual commitments

### 4.4 Dynamic Pricing

**Student Discounts**:
- Early bird: 50% off first 100 users (₩4,950/mo)
- Referral: 1 month free for both referrer and referee
- Student verification: 20% off with .edu email

**Seasonal Promotions**:
- Back to school (March): 30% off first 3 months
- Summer intensive (July): Buy 3 months, get 1 free
- Black Friday (November): 40% off annual plans

### 5.2 Sales Process

#### **B2C (Self-Serve)**:
1. User discovers platform (ad, referral, organic)
2. Signs up for free account
3. Completes onboarding tutorial
4. Uses free features for 2-4 weeks
5. Receives upgrade prompts (in-app, email)
6. Converts to paid plan

**Conversion Rate Target**: 10%

#### **B2B (Enterprise Sales)**:
1. Identify decision makers (dean, department head)
2. Initial meeting (value proposition, demo)
3. Proof of concept (pilot with 50-100 students)
4. Evaluation period (3 months)
5. Proposal and negotiation
6. Contract signing
7. Implementation and onboarding

**Sales Cycle**: 3-6 months
**Close Rate**: 30%

## 6. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

### 6.1 LTV Calculation

**Premium User LTV**:
```
Average subscription length: 12 months
Monthly price: ₩9,900
Gross margin: 80%

LTV = 12 × ₩9,900 × 0.8 = ₩95,040
```

**Pro User LTV**:
```
Average subscription length: 18 months
Monthly price: ₩29,900
Gross margin: 80%

LTV = 18 × ₩29,900 × 0.8 = ₩430,560
```

**Weighted Average LTV**:
```
(80% Premium + 20% Pro)
= (0.8 × ₩95,040) + (0.2 × ₩430,560)
= ₩76,032 + ₩86,112
= ₩162,144
```

**B2B University LTV**:
```
Average contract length: 36 months
Annual price: ₩30M
Gross margin: 60%

LTV = 3 × ₩30M × 0.6 = ₩54M
```

### 6.2 LTV Improvement Strategies

1. **Increase Retention**:
   - Improve onboarding (reduce time to value)
   - Regular feature updates
   - Community engagement
   - Personalized learning paths

2. **Increase Revenue Per User**:
   - Upsell Pro features (AI assistant, custom domains)
   - Cross-sell additional services (mentoring, courses)
   - Add-ons (extra storage, compute credits)

3. **Reduce Churn**:
   - Proactive customer success
   - Health score monitoring
   - Win-back campaigns
   - Feedback loops
